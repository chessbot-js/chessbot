Introducing

This automated computer program worked as Google Chrome extension. You can install them into your browser then go to Chess.com or lichess.org log in or register new account and play.

How to play

When you start playing new game (online or live mode) you will see a robot icon (position defers on playing mode). Right from them you will see best next move. Chess engine working in background and you will see new suggestion after few times.
